Common Scam Indicators

  • Upfront payment or registration fees
  • Unrealistic salary promises for no experience
  • No interview process or immediate joining pressure
  • Vague job descriptions with generic responsibilities
  • Requests for personal banking information
  • Communication from free email domains
  • Cryptocurrency or get-rich-quick schemes

Safety Tips

Never pay money to get a job or internship

Research the company on LinkedIn and Glassdoor

Verify email addresses and domain names

Trust your instincts - if it sounds too good to be true, it probably is

Never share your bank details or ID proofs upfront
